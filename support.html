Common Questions

Apple Health says "No data today" but I have steps. Open iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Life Clock and confirm steps, exercise, sleep, and resting heart rate are toggled on for reading. Life Clock cannot read what you haven't shared.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription? iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; you keep Pro access until then.

How do I get a refund? Apple is the merchant of record for all Life Clock subscriptions. Submit a refund request at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I delete all my data? In the app: Profile → Delete all data. Because Life Clock is local-first and we have no server, this removes everything. There is nothing left for us to delete on our side.

The app feels too heavy / mortality-focused. Open Profile → If this app is making you anxious. You can switch to Gentle tone, hide the projected-age display in favor of "time earned today," or get crisis-resource phone numbers.

I'm under 18 and I don't see the alcohol/smoking pickers. That's intentional. Life Clock's age-gated UI hides those questions for users who report a date of birth making them under 18.

Mental-Health Resources

If Life Clock's framing is causing distress, please reach out:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (US) — call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line (US/UK/Ireland/Canada) — text HOME to 741741
  • International: findahelpline.com

Reporting a Bug

Email ksakhakorn@gmail.com with:

  • What you did (screen, action)
  • What happened
  • What you expected to happen
  • iOS version and iPhone model (Settings → General → About)

If you can attach a screenshot, that almost always helps.
